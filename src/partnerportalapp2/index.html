Now Available

The new PartnerPortal mobile app from EnerBank USA® gives contractors convenient access to their customers’ home improvement loans from anywhere!

Start Growing Your Business the Smart Way

Mobile App

To access the EnerBank PartnerPortal app, just use your current PartnerPortal username and password. (You must be enrolled in ExpressSign to use the app.)

PartnerPortal Mobile Loan Application Features

Search, filter and manage all customer loan applications from a single dashboard

Convenience and Confidence

When you access customer loan applications through the app, you save time and impress your clients.

Funds in a Flash

Request loan funds through the app, and your customers can approve via text or phone.

Cash Flow

Qualified EnerBank contractors can request multiple disbursements for their projects.

Roll with the Punches

Job scope changed? Customer wants to upgrade? No problem! You can use the app to request on-the-fly updates up to the expected amount.

Knowledge is Power

Updated tracking features let you filter projects by application date, expiration date, or even salesperson — then view full details. You can also filter by loan status as applications move through funding
