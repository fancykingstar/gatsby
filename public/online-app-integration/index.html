Online Loan Application Integration

Add a button to your website to seamlessly provide your customers with a quick and easy way to apply for a loan.

Check out the loan option buttons below and follow the simple instructions to get your own personalized button complete with your loan by phone number, contractor ID, and loan code. If you'd like to request a button for another loan product or a custom design, send us an email with your request and we will respond within a couple of days with your custom button.

Follow these steps to add the button to your website today!

1

Use the form below to enter your Program Phone Number, Contractor ID, the loan code of the product you would like to offer (this can be found on your Reference Guide or in your PartnerPortal account).

2

Select the corresponding loan button below the form, then choose from our most popular loan products including Same-As-Cash, and Reduced Interest loans.

3

Copy and paste the embedded code onto your website, or if you choose to design your own button, select the text link and paste it onto your website.

You're all set. Your customers can now begin their application directly from your website!
